About Jesus Discipleship Ministry

Founded in 1990, serving our community with faith, hope, and love

Our Story

Jesus Discipleship Ministry was founded in 1990 with a vision to create a vibrant, Spirit-filled community where believers are equipped to live out their faith boldly and share the love of Christ. Located in Mokola, Ibadan, Nigeria, we have been serving our community and reaching out to the lost for over a decade.

Our Mission

Winning the lost, discipling the converts unto spiritual maturity, building able leaders to defend the integrity of the sound gospel.

Our Vision

To be a vibrant, Spirit-filled community where believers are equipped to live out their faith boldly and share the love of Christ.
